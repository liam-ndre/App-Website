About Us.

We Built EcoBoi for the World

We'd like the task of 'lessening the damage' to not look so daunting and to be almost seemless. We want you to have saving the environment look almost as easy as breathing.

Our Story

Ecoboi is about our drive for the love of the environment. We’re a young team with big ambitions, taking small steps. Our vision to reach such a goal lay behind the foundation of this application. I hope you can see that vision through our application :)
